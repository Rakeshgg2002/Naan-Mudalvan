STORY BOARD

In the 2019 Lok Sabha elections, a comprehensive quantitative analysis revealed that candidates with criminal records were on the rise, underscoring concerns about the nexus between politics and crime. Additionally, gender diversity remained a challenge, with a significant underrepresentation of female candidates. Lastly, the data highlighted the importance of factors such as campaign expenditures and political party affiliations in predicting election outcomes.
